Our story and mission

Welcome to RELEVENTS. We are the next generation event curator that delivers contextual experiences for advancing who we are and what we do. We invite the best of breed to collaborate, curate and innovate to deliver huge, meaningful impact. By prioritizing context over content, our events are real and produce actionable results. We strive to achieve just the right balance of social and financial value creation. Alternative by design, Relevents centers on continuous professional development and cross-pollination in a peer-to-peer economy. Focused on 3 core communities: innovation, insights and brand strategy/design, Relevents designs a suite of offerings to accelerate, challenge and transform the most seasoned people into leaders in all aspects of their life.
Simply put, we deliver real events for what matters with who matters.

Our Culture

You will never have coffee with the CEO and you will never be the CEO. That’s because there is no CEO. Relevents promotes less hierarchy and more collaboration, where everyone’s voice matters. You will never be called into HR because there is no HR. There are benefits but no people police. If there’s an issue, we talk about it. With our proprietary Reciprocity pricing model ™ we build social value at the same rate of financial value – resetting KPIs and redefining what it means to be in the event business.
